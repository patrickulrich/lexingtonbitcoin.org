Bitcoin Classes

If you're looking for structured sets of lecture materials and challenges, one of the many online courses may be helpful. Most of the online courses are free or fairly low cost.


Non-technical Online Classes:

  • Bitcoin Video Course for Non-Technical Newbies
  • 10 Day Email Course: Basics of Bitcoin
  • Binance Academy
  • Bitcoin for Everybody (Saylor Academy)
  • Blockchain & Money (MIT)
  • Crypto Startup School
  • Learn Me A Bitcoin Lessons
  • Introduction to Bitcoin (create a free trial)
  • Ministry of Nodes

Technical Online Classes:

  • Bitcoin 101 Blackboard Series (7+ hours of tutorials)
  • Bitcoin & Cryptocurrency Technologies (Princeton)
  • Bitcoin for Developers (Saylor Academy)
  • Bitcoin Programming 101 (Ivan on Tech)
  • Bitcoin Protocol Development Curriculum (Chaincode Labs)
  • Blockchain at Berkeley
  • Chaincode Online Seminars
  • Coursera Cryptocurrency Course (61 videos in 11 sections)
  • Introduction to Digital Currency - MOOC offered by University of Nicosia
  • Khan Academy Bitcoin Course (9 ~10 minute videos)
  • MIT DCI's Open Courses
  • Mooniversity
  • TeachBitcoin with Libbitcoin
  • Udemy Course (over 8 hours of video tutorials)

Technical Offline Classes:

  • 21 Lectures
  • Blockchain Training Conference
  • Chaincode Residency
  • Pleb.fi
  • Programming Blockchain

Knowledge Tests:

  • Cryptocurrency Certification Consortium
